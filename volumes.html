Computation of Volume

For computation of the volume of earth work,the sectional areas of the cross-section which are taken transverse to the longitudinal section during profile levelling are first calculated.

After calculation of cross-sectional areas,the volume of earth work is calculated by :

  1. the trapezoidal(or averagee end area) rule,and
  2. the prismoidal rule.

Notes :

  1. The prismoidal rule gives the correct volume directly.
  2. The trapezoidal rule does not give the correct volume.Prismodal correction should be applied for this purpose.This correction is always subtractive.
  3. Cutting is denoted by a positive sign and filling by a negative sign.

  1. Level Section

    Cross-sectional area = {(b+b+2sh)/2}×h
    = (b+sh)×h

Formula for calculation of volume :

  1. Trapezoidal Rule(Average End Area Rule):

    2. Volume(cutting or filling), V =D/2{A1+An+2(A2+A3+A4......+An-1)}

    i.e., volume = common distance/2 {area of 1st section + area of last section + 2(sum of area of other sections)}

  2. Prismoidal Formula :

    3. Volume(cutting or filling), V =D/3{A1+An+4(A2+A4......+An-1)+2(A3+A5+A7.....+An-2)}

    i.e., volume = common distance/3 {area of 1st section + area of last section + 4(sum of area of even sections)+2(sum of area of odd sections)}
